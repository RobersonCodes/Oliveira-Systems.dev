Problem

Public health management demands precision, security, and scale.

Health units manage patients, professionals, appointments, and sensitive records. Without a centralized, secure platform, care delivery becomes slow, error-prone, and impossible to audit.

Solution

A robust API centralizing every public-health workflow.

Built with layered architecture, JWT authentication, relational persistence, and business-rule enforcement — with integration hooks for e-SUS APS, RNDS, and AI-driven triage modules.

Problem

Downtime costs money. Slow detection makes it worse.

Enterprises need to detect failures in seconds, track service availability across distributed systems, and respond to incidents before they cascade into user-facing outages.

Solution

Continuous monitoring with automated incident response.

Guardian Recovery polls services in real time, runs health checks, measures response latency, and automatically opens incidents when services degrade — reducing MTTR dramatically.
